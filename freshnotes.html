    • Fresh Fragrance Notes

    A vibrant breeze—crisp, invigorating, and refreshingly pure. Experience the lively spirit of perfumery’s freshest notes with Scentvoy.

    A Burst of Freshness

    Fresh fragrances capture the invigorating spirit of nature’s zest. They evoke memories of crisp morning air, sunlit citrus groves, and gentle ocean breezes. At Scentvoy, we celebrate three icons of the fresh world—Citrus, Oceanic, and Green notes—each bringing a vibrant energy to your olfactory journey.

    Citrus

    Bright · Zesty · Refreshing

    Bursting with vibrant energy, citrus notes awaken the senses with their zesty and invigorating aroma. Bright and crisp, they deliver a fresh, uplifting burst reminiscent of sunlit orchards and sparkling morning dew.

    Oceanic

    Refreshing · Breezy · Marine

    Evoking the crisp morning sea breeze, ocean notes bring a refreshing and invigorating freshness. Clean, airy, and subtly salty, they capture the essence of endless waves and open horizons.

    Green

    Crisp · Verdant · Energizing

    Vibrant and fresh, green notes bring the essence of lush foliage and sunlit leaves. Their crisp and natural character breathes life into any fragrance, evoking the vitality of a serene forest glade.

    Fresh Accord Pyramid

    Top Notes | Zesty Citrus · Sparkling Marine
    Heart Notes | Ocean Breeze · Crisp Green Leaves
    Base Notes | Soft Musk · Driftwood · Clean Amber

    Crafting Your Signature

    Whether you’re drawn to the zesty burst of citrus, the invigorating whisper of ocean breeze, or the crisp vitality of green leaves,
    Scentvoy’s curated edit invites you to discover a fragrance that refreshes and energizes your unique story.
