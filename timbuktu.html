    L'Artisan Parfumeur Timbuktu

    $210.00

    Ancient mysticism combined with profound modernity. A fragrance inspired by Wusulan, the ritual performed by Malian women to scent their hair and skin, with flower roots, balms and spices. Smoky papyrus, incense and patchouli pay tribute to the perfume’s latin origin, “per fumum”: “through smoke”.

    Scent Notes

    Notes: Pink Pepper, Karo-Kaoundé, Vetiver
